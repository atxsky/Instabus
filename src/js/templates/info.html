Frequently Asked Questions

Is Instabus open source?

Instabus is open source and licensed under the GNU GPL V3 License. The source is available at github.com/luqmaan/Instabus.

What is Instabus?

Instabus is a webapp that shows you where your bus is in realtime.

Why is the vehicle location out of date?

CapMetro only updates vehicle locations every 90 seconds, except for MetroRapid vehicles which are updated every 60seconds. You should encourage CapMetro to provide more accurate realtime data.
